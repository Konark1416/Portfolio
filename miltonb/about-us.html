About Us

  The Story Of Milton B

Milton B was established in the summer of 2017 by Jack Ahmed. The cafeteria is a vibrant and innovative culinary destination nestled at 3498 Avenue du Parc in Montreal.

The Milton B cafe is located in the heart of the city of Montreal. Come to Milton B for a study session or for a hearty breakfast, brunch and more. We’re waiting for you 24/7, open 365 days to serve you the best coffee, dessert, bakery in Montreal. And also, quality food eateries  such as Poutine, Pizza.

A quality brunch cafe in Montreal, we also believe in nurturing both community and nature for a healthier planet. We’re a cafe MTL that’s more than a cute cafe. We believe in delivering excellence and an experience. And if you’re a student in Downtown Montreal, we’re a cafe near you. Come pay us a visit for coffee, and more.

Our Staff

At Milton B Café, our staff is the heartbeat of our cozy, community-focused space. Our talented baristas in Montreal are dedicated to perfecting every cup of coffee. They  blend their deep knowledge of beans, roasting techniques, and brewing methods. All this to deliver a drink that’s as delightful to the eye as it is to the palate. Our culinary team works with the freshest, locally sourced ingredients in the kitchen. So, to craft a menu full of delicious pastries, sandwiches, and salads that satisfy with every bite.

Our team is committed to creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like part of the family. Everyone at Milton B Café is passionate about making your visit memorable. From the friendly faces at the counter to the chefs in the kitchen. We’re proud to be a part of the community, supporting local artists and businesses. The best cafe in Montreal is  always striving to make its neighborhood a better place. Rest assured, we’re more than good coffee, or cafe latte.
